Shipping & Delivery Information

Shipping Options

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - $5.99
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - $12.99
  • Free Shipping: On orders over $50 (5-7 business days)

Processing Time

Processing of your order usually takes 1-2 business days (excluding both weekends and holidays). You will be informed through an email when the order leaves the warehouse.

Order Tracking

After the package is sent, you will obtain a tracking number so you can follow its delivery to you.

International Shipping

We have worldwide shipping. Items are shipped internationally and normally arrive in 7-14 business days. Your country may add customs fees to your shipment.
