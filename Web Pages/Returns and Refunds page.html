Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Policy

Most of what we sell can be returned within a 30-day time period. If you are unhappy with what you got, you can return it for a refund or an exchange.

How to Return

  1. Contact customer service within 30 days after you get your order
  2. Wrap the product in its original pack and include all the necessary bits
  3. Attach the original document showing the order or receipt
  4. Send the product back to our return center

Refund Process

  • When you return something, a refund can be processed inside 5 business days of us receiving it
  • The shipping costs that you first pay are not refundable
  • A refund is given back to the initial payment method

Conditions

  • Product has to be unused and in its original form
  • The product must be in its original packaging with all the tags
  • Software, personalized goods and perishable items cannot be returned to the shop
